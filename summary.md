# Summary of ECB Press Conference Analysis
## Chosen ECB page
I chose the ECB monetary policy statement and press conference page from 30 October 2025:
https://www.ecb.europa.eu/press/press_conference/monetary-policy-statement/2025/html/ecb.is251030~4f74dde15e.en.html
## Sentiment package used
For the sentiment analysis, I used TextBlob. I chose it because it is simple to apply to short text units specially paragraphs and it provides a polarity score that can be classified into positive, neutral, or negative labels.
## Paragraph-level results
The paragraph-level results suggest that the overall tone of the press conference is mostly neutral, with some positive or negative paragraphs depending on the topic. This is expected since central bank communication is usually formal.  The most frequent words and the word cloud show that the main topic focus is monetary policy, inflation, economic activity, interest rates, and the euro area.
